Spotify Socialize
Tired of looking through your friends' playlists to see what songs you both like? Spotify Socialize will let you log in with your Spotify account and select a user to see what songs and artists you have in common. This is perfect for making party or group playlists, where everyone will be able to listen to songs that they like.
